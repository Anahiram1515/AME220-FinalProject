1990s - Pleated Skirt

A skirt with vertical creases, or pleats, that are stitched or pressed into the fabric to create a structured, voluminous look that allows for graceful movement. Some celebrities in this era using pencil skirts are Clueless, Jennifer Aniston, and supermodel Naomi Campbell.
